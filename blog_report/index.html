The Catalyst

VS

Blind Below Feeders

Monitoring stops at the substation level. Individual pole conditions remain a complete black box.

Reactive Nightmares

Crews dispatched blindly after public complaints. Night-time restrictions add hours to resolution.

20% AT&C Losses

Massive power lost to theft & inefficiency. Discovered only months later via audits. Hundreds of crores wasted.

₹3-8L Transformer Burns

Thousands of transformer failures per year, each costing lakhs. No early warning system exists.

Pole-Level Intelligence

IoT Edge nodes on every pole capturing Voltage, Current, and Temperature every 5 seconds.

Predictive AI Maintenance

Weibull survival models forecast degradation. Auto-generates work orders before failure occurs.

Automated Revenue Recovery

Differential analysis (Feeder Input vs Pole Sum) pinpoints theft zones geographically in real-time.

Edge-Based Auto Protection

Solid-State Relay trips locally on critical faults — no cloud dependency needed for safety.
